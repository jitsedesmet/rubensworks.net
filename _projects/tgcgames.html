TGC Games (The Game Collection) was a Flash gaming website. On average, it had 1000 to 1500 daily visitors that could play thousands of Flash games.

The website had a PHP back-end that was built with the Yii framework. New games were automatically fed into the website from distribution sources like Mochi Media and FlashGameDistribution.
