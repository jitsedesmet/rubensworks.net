Allectro Radio was an internet radio focusing on electronic music. At its peak, the radio reached 300 to 400 listening hours per day from people all over the world.

A website for the radio was available that was written in PHP using Yii framework. It showed which track was currently playing, a list of upcoming tracks and the complete playlist for the coming week. It was listed on several internet radio collections like TuneIn, on which there were over 12.700 followers. The Facebook page and Twitter account are still available. Applications were created for both Android and iOS.
